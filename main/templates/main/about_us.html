About us

Movies.kz is one of the largest legal online cinemas in Kazakhstan, we have been successfully operating since 2021.

Our catalog contains over 70,000 films, cartoons and serials. Since its launch, the service has been visited by over 20 million users. The monthly audience is 0.8 million people.

Movies.kz is available on most smart TVs (Samsung, Sony, LG, Philips, Panasonic and other popular models), game consoles (PlayStation 3, 4 and 5 and Series X | S) and mobile devices (Android and iOS) ... Download movies and TV series to mobile devices and watch anywhere in the world, even without the Internet.



Movies.kz - advanced sound and image technologies

Our technologies allow you to create the picture and sound quality in your home that will give you the feeling of theatrical viewing. Movies.kz is the first online cinema in Kazakhstan, which gave viewers the opportunity to watch movies with Dolby Atmos and Dolby Digital Plus sound. Watch movies in HDR, 3D, Ultra HD 4K and 8K quality. No ads, no distractions - just an immersion in the world of cinema!
